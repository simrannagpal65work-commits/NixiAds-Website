FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know about Nixi.

What is Nixi and how does it work?

Nixi is an AI-powered ad creative generator built for D2C brands and marketers. You enter your product URL, choose a campaign angle, and Nixi generates complete, brand-aware ad creatives — including visual concept, headline, body copy, and CTA — ready to run on Meta and Instagram. No designer or creative brief needed.

How is Nixi different from Canva or other design tools?

Canva and similar tools give you templates — you still design the ad yourself. Nixi generates complete, campaign-ready ad creatives from scratch. It understands your brand identity, product, and audience, then produces structured ads with performance-oriented copy and visuals. Zero design skills required.

Do I need a designer or prompt-writing skills to use Nixi?

No. Nixi requires zero design skills and zero prompt writing. You bring the product URL — Nixi reads your brand automatically and handles the creative strategy, visual direction, and copywriting.

What ad formats does Nixi support?

Nixi generates ads in all major Meta and Instagram formats — Square, Portrait, Stories & Reels, and Landscape. Every output is formatted to the correct dimensions and text-safe zones for its platform.

How long does it take to generate an ad with Nixi?

Once Nixi launches, the entire flow — brand scan, angle selection, and ad generation — is designed to take under 3 minutes.

Is Nixi suitable for D2C brands on Meta and Instagram?

Yes. Nixi is built specifically for D2C brands, e-commerce founders, and performance marketers running ads on Meta and Instagram. It understands product categories like fashion, skincare, footwear, food, and electronics.

Does Nixi understand my brand identity automatically?

Yes. Nixi's Brand Sense feature scans your brand website and extracts your visual DNA, tone of voice, colour palette, audience demographics, buying motivations, and competitor landscape. Every ad it generates uses this data — so your creatives always feel on-brand, not generic.
