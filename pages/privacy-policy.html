Last updated: May 2026  ·  Applies to nixiads.com and all associated services

Nixi ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it when you use the Nixi platform.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Nixi, we collect:

  • Account details: name, email address, and authentication credentials
  • Brand and product data: URLs, images, and information you provide to generate ad creatives
  • Usage data: features used, generation history, and credit consumption
  • Payment information: billing details processed by our payment provider
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and referring URL

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Nixi platform

We will never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. AI Processing and Third-Party Models

Nixi uses third-party AI providers to generate ad creatives. When you generate an ad, relevant inputs such as product details and brand information are sent to these providers. This data is processed under their respective data processing agreements. We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

4. Payment Data

Payments are processed by a third-party payment provider. We do not store card details on our servers. We retain transaction records solely for billing and refund purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices. We retain your account and generated content for as long as your account is active or as required by law. Upon account closure, your data will be deleted within 30 days unless legal retention obligations apply.

6. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our platform. These tools collect anonymised data and do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, withdraw consent to marketing communications, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@nixiads.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted their data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
